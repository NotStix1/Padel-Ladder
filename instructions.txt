Build a mobile-first Padel Ladder web app.
Goal: let players join courts, challenge each other, record results, and climb ladders. Each court has its own public challenges feed and ladder.

Users & Roles
- Player (default)
- Court Owner/Admin (owns one or more courts; manages ladders, rules, and moderation)

Auth
- Email/password + social (Google/Apple)
- Profile: name, avatar, preferred hand/level, home court(s)

Courts
- Any user can register a padel court → becomes Owner
- Court profile: name, location, hours, rules, contact, court images
- Court settings: enable/disable ladder(s), match formats, scoring, max challenge distance, inactivity rules, season dates

Ladders
- Per court (support multiple ladders per court: Open/Mixed/Women/Men)
- Ranking model: configurable (Elo/Glicko or classic swap-on-win)
- Placement: new players start at bottom or provisional ranking
- Decay/inactivity options (auto drop after X days)

Challenges (core flow)
1. Player selects court + ladder, taps “Challenge”
2. Can challenge up to N spots above (configurable)
3. Opponent accepts/declines within T hours (auto-cancel on timeout)
4. Match scheduled: date/time, optional court booking link
5. After match, both players upload final score (set-by-set); either can submit, both must confirm; tie-breaks supported
6. Disputes: Owner/Admin can resolve/override

Results & Visibility
- Each court has a Challenges page/feed: upcoming, in-progress, completed matches
- Each completed match shows players, score, ranking change
- Ladder page shows positions, streaks, recent activity, last played date

Player Experience
- Join/leave ladder, view challenge eligibility, recent opponents
- Quick rematch, match history, win/loss, set stats
- Optional skill estimate and suggested opponents

Notifications
- Push/email: challenge received, accepted, reminders, result needed, ranking change
- Court broadcast (owners): rule updates, season resets

Admin/Owner Tools
- Edit court profile & ladder rules
- Approve/ban players from a court
- Season management: start/end, reset or carry-over ratings, publish champions
- Analytics: participation, matches/week, top movers

Fair Play / Anti-abuse
- Cooldowns, no-repeat limits, forfeit rules, auto-expiry on unplayed matches

Search & Discovery
- Find courts by location
- Join ladders with one tap; request access if court is private

Tech & UX
- Mobile-first PWA (installable), offline for viewing ladders & scheduled matches
- Fast list views, skeleton loaders, infinite scroll
- Accessibility AA, dark mode

Data (high level)
- Users, Courts, Ladders, Memberships, Challenges, Matches, Ratings, Seasons, Notifications, AuditLog

APIs (high level)
- `/auth/*`, `/courts/*`, `/ladders/*`, `/ladders/{id}/members`, `/challenges/*`, `/matches/*`, `/ratings/*`, `/seasons/*`, `/admin/*`

Acceptance Criteria
- A player can register/login, join a court’s ladder, challenge someone above, record a score, and see the ladder update immediately.
- Court’s Challenges page shows that match before and after it’s played.
- Owners can configure rules and resolve disputes.

Nice-to-haves
- Court booking integration link, QR share for court ladder, CSV export of ladder.
